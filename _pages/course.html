Course Goals

The course will explore topics in Computer Aided Geometric Design, with an emphasis on the following:

  • Parametric curves and surfaces
  • Subdivision methods
  • Knot theory

Texts

参考书籍包括但不限于：

  • Gerald Farin, N. Sapidis, E. Schuster. Computer Aided Geometric Design, A K Peters/CRC Press, 1996. Download
