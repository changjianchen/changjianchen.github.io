I am a final-year Ph.D. student in the School of Software at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Shixia Liu. My research interest focuses on visual analytics and machine learning, especially visual analysis methods to improve training data quality. Prior to Tsinghua University, I reveived my B.S. in Electronic Engineering from University of Science and Technology of China.
